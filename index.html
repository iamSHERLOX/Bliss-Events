About Us

We are a group of undergraduates who wish to help you in planning your event with us. We hope to support you in planning your event all in one place easily and make you special day more beautiful.


So the adventure begins…


If you’re here, then you’re in the right place, and it’s time to make the magic happen. Bliss events is a event planning company based in Colombo - Sri Lanka striving to create your day that is effortlessly and wholly represents who you are, reflecting your personality and your great taste.

“What you need, is an Event, to remember for a lifetime.”
